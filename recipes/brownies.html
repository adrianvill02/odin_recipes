Brownies

Brownies are a delectable dessert loved by many. Overwhelmed by chocolate, it is hard not to love them.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Salt
  • Vanilla extract
  • Eggs
  • All purpose flower
  • Pecan

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
  2. Combine the butter, sugar, cocoa, and salt in a medium heatproof bowl.
  3. Set the bowl in a barely simmering skillet of water.
  4. Stir vanilla with a wooden spoon and add eggs on at a time, stirring vigorously
  5. Stir in nuts and spread in an evenly line pan.
  6. Bake until a toothpick plunged into the center emerges sliughtly moist with batter.
