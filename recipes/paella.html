Paella Recipe

Paella is a classic spanish dish made with rice, saffron, vegetables, chicken, and seafood.

It is traditionally all cooked in one pan.

Ingredients

  • rice
  • saffron
  • vegetables
  • chicken
  • seafood

Notes

  • Use short grain rize and an appropriate pan.
  • Broth is crucial
  • Spanish or Portuguese Chorizo ONLY
  • You need a large pan, and a heat source enough to fill the bottome of the pan
  • El soarrat. Means the crusting of the rice and spices at the bottom of the pan into a pie crust type texture.

Instructions

  1. Cook the chicken with oil and garlic.
  2. Fry the saffron in wine.
  3. Cook the broth.
  4. Cook the rice.
  5. Reduce heat and let it reduce.
